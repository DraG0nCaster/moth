Research Simulation

This is a simulation for biological scientific research. Author: Damil Ginatullin

Simulation rules

The simulation consists of a population of individuals represented as moths on a canvas. Each individual starts with random color, and they move randomly within the canvas. Background of the simulation changes color in the a certain time interval which you can set before the simulation. When an individual is clicked, it is removed from the simulation, then a new individual is created in a random position. This individual is child of random alive individual. Children inherit the color of their parent and this color has a chance to mutate depending on the parent's mutation rate. Mutation rate is also inhereted from the parent and has a small chance to change. One simulation lasts for 3 minutes.

Your goal is to click on as many individuals as possible until the simulation ends.
